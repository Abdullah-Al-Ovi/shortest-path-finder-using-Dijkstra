Instructions

  • Click anywhere on the screen to create a station.
  • After you finished adding stations, start adding routes.
  • Click button "Add routes" before adding routes.
  • Routes can be added by clicking one station and then another station.
  • All routes' have default weight scaled to the size between them.
  • You can click on the existing weight and change it according to the need.
  • Enter the Initial station in input box and click "Go" to find minimum cost path.
  • Before clicking Go, make sure all weights are as you need them to be.
